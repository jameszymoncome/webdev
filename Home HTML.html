Mission/Vision

The CNSC Emergency Response Team (ERT) aims to protect human life, prevent harm, and minimize damage during emergencies like unexpected injuries during intercollegiate meet, and other requests from different events that needs first aid. The objectives include emergency assessment, life safety, incident control, and debriefing. The organization employ tactics like rapid response, clear communication, and proper training.
